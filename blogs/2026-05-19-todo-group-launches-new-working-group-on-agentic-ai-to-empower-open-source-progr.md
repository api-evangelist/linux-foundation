To explore AI agent workflows and the role of open source management The TODO Group is launching a new Agentic AI to Empower OSPOs Working Group , a community-led effort for Open Source Program Office (OSPO) practitioners and open source management professionals to explore how AI agents are beginning to shape the day-to-day work of OSPOs.
